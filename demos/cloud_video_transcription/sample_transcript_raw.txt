Welcome back to the applied learning seminar. Today we are connecting source
materials to a concept index and a coverage report.

The first step is to build a manafest of the source files so every transformed
artifact can be traced back to the original input. We should keep the video
files private and only promote reviewed summaries or synthetic examples.

Next, split the audio into predictable segments. If the transcription model is
uncertain, keep the [unclear] marker instead of inventing a term. Then clean
obvious spelling errors such as manafest and retrival.

Finally, the analyst reviews the cleaned transcript excerpt before it becomes
part of a downstream retrival or reporting workflow.
